My Story

For nearly eight years, I've built my career in consulting at a Big Four firm, leading complex projects for Fortune 100 clients. At the same time, I've always been the planner in my personal life, the one friends and family turned to for organizing celebrations, coordinating travel, and bringing people together.

Those two worlds share more in common than you might think. My consulting career taught me how to anticipate challenges, manage countless moving pieces, communicate with clarity, and execute every detail with precision. It's an approach that has become the foundation of how I plan every celebration.

That passion came full circle while planning my own destination wedding in Portugal. Seeing months of thoughtful planning transform into an unforgettable weekend for the people we love inspired me to launch Ives & Ivory Events.

More than anything, I'm passionate about the art of creating celebrations that feel effortless, intentional, and unforgettable. I love thoughtfully designing the flow of an event, from the timeline and guest experience to the meaningful moments that become lifelong memories. I'm continually inspired by the creativity and innovation within the events industry, always seeking fresh ideas that elevate each celebration while remaining timeless and true to my clients.

Today, I combine the project management expertise I've developed throughout my career with a genuine passion for creating celebrations that are intentional, seamless, and deeply meaningful, so you can simply enjoy the moment.
